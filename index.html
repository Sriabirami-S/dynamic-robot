About GO1

Intelligent Quadruped Robot

The Unitree GO1 is a high-performance quadruped robot developed for robotics research, artificial intelligence, autonomous navigation, and educational applications.

It combines agile locomotion, intelligent perception, and ROS compatibility, making it an ideal platform for developers, researchers, and robotics enthusiasts.

Key Features

Powerful capabilities that make the Unitree GO1 an ideal platform for robotics research and intelligent applications.

High-Speed Mobility

Capable of agile locomotion with excellent stability, allowing smooth movement across different terrains.

AI & ROS Ready

Fully compatible with ROS and AI development, making it suitable for autonomous robotics, computer vision, and research.

Advanced Perception

Supports intelligent sensing and navigation, enabling real-time environmental awareness and autonomous operation.
